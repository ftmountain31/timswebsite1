comedy




tim performs improv, standup, and sketch comedy in a little city you may have heard of called new york city. scroll down for some samples of tim making you laugh your whole frickin' butt off.





rugs improv




here you can watch rugs' zoom livestream show from april 3rd, 2020. rugs is carsen smith, kevin o'malley, emma carey, will braithwaite, winnie stack, andy vega, and tim. they are all beautiful and amazing.

every month, they perform "improvised antiques roadshow" where they improvise scenes based on appraisals of items taken from the audience. find rugs on instagram for more information about upcoming shows.

also, check out this youtube playlist for more rugs content.




big knife, small task




big knife, small task is tim's hit web show in which he attempts to perform a small task with a big knife. the show features beloved segments such as "small knife, big task," in which he attempts to perform a big task with a small knife.

many of these videos were filmed by kate chapman, tim's good friend. others were filmed by aidan mountain, tim's only brother.

featured is the most recent episode. you can watch the rest of season 1 on youtube right here.

other videos




"lisa, darling" is a sketch written by and starring alex brennan, kevin o'malley, erin kiernan, and tim. they are close friends of tim's and he loves them very much.






"benji, the boy who has never done anything" was charlie murray, kevin o'malley, and tim's submission to campus movie fest in 2018. they placed in the top 16, and charie won the "golden tripod" award for best actor, which if you know anything at all, is basically the equivalent of an oscar for the greater new york area's second-or-third-largest mid-tier collegiate film festival.






"tobey" was tim and charlie's 2019 campus movie fest submission. this one got totally snubbed.
