music




tim has been writing and performing music for his entire life. what started as a penchant for pop piano ballads as a child had evolved by college into an obsession with production, electronics, and eclectic styles. below are some of his recent projects.





"buzz along with me" music video




on april 3rd, 2020, tim released the music video for "buzz along with me," the third track from his most recent album. the video was shot, edited, and produced by tim's cool, funny, and nice friend, emma carey.

check out tim's youtube channel here.




hand my hold my umm hand




in october 2019, tim released his debut, self-produced full-length album, hand my hold my umm hand, an exploration of how cute and scary love is. the beautiful cover art was designed by tim's good friend sarah stinson.

(the above link takes you to spotify. you can also stream the album on apple music, bandcamp, youtube, and wherever else you stream music.)





touch pool

since the summer of 2018, tim has been recording and performing fuzzy psych-punk under the moniker "touch pool". you can stream touch pool's debut album, bed, on spotify, apple music, bandcamp, youtube, or wherever else you stream music.

the album artwork was designed by dylan kennedy, tim's amazing and cool friend.
