writing




tim's love of writing encompasses poetry, prose, drama, and, more recently, writing for the screen. published works and upcoming projects will be posted here sporadically.


to request a sample of tim's writing in any medium, please contact him at timmountain2@gmail.com.





plays




all inclusive is a play tim wrote with kevin o'malley and emma carey. it was produced by fordham experimental theatre and premiered in fordham university's blackbox in march 2019.

if the embedded video does not work, click here to watch.






"owe, my heart" is a one-act musical that tim wrote and presented as part of fordham experimental theatre's playwright's festival in march 2018.

the pit band was comprised of phil thompson on keyboards, david prost on drums, ford murphy on bass, and tim on guitar.






"home sweet home depot" is a one-act musical, and was tim's submission to fordham experimental theatre's 2017 playwright's festival.
